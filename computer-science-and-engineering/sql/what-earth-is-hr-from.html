What earth is HR from?

(A) Earth 1

(B) Earth 38

(C) Earth 3

(D) ** Earth 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In actuality he was an Author on Earth-19, and the public image of his Earth’s STAR Labs. However he was revealed to be a fraud and with the help of his partner, Randolf Morgan, HR came to Earth one, beliving he could start fresh.

Concept note-2: -H.R. was released as Cisco fought as his champion and won the trial by combat. As a result, he agreed to never return to Earth-19. Due to feeling held back, Wally West would enlist the help of H.R. to train him in his new speedster abilities.

Concept note-3: -"Accelerated Man” is a speedster native to Earth-19, and an overseer of the law-enforcing Collectors of Earth-19, formerly including Cynthia/Gypsy. Due to the destruction and recreation of the multiverse, his status is unknown.

Concept note-4: -In the DC comics, the Earth-19 equivalent of The Flash is known as the Accelerated Man.

Concept note-5: -Harrison Sherloque Wells (/ˈʃɜːrloʊk/), a detective from Earth-221, is hired in season five by Team Flash to investigate the mystery of the metahuman serial killer Cicada.

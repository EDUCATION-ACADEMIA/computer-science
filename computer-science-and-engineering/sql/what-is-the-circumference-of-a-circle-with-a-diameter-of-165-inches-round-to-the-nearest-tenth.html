What is the circumference of a circle with a diameter of 16.5 inches? Round to the nearest tenth.

(A) 103.62

(B) 33

(C) ** 51.81

(D) 25.91

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To calculate the circumference of a circle, multiply the diameter of the circle with (pi). The circumference can also be calculated by multiplying 2×radius with pi (=3.14).

Concept note-2: -Thus, the circumference of the circle whose diameter is 16 cm is 50.28 cm.

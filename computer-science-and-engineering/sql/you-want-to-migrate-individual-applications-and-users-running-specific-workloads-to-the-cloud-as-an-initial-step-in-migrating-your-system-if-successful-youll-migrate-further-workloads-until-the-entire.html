(A) Perform an online migration of the database

(B) ** Follow a piecemeal approach to migration.

(C) Perform an offline migration of the database

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are many methods for migrating to AWS-however, the lift and shift approach remains the quickest, simplest, lowest-risk and most cost-effective way to get working in the cloud. The lift and shift migration approach involves migrating your application and connected data to the cloud with little or no changes.

Concept note-2: -For many applications, the best approach is to rapidly move to cloud and then rearchitect in AWS. You can use AWS Application Migration Service (MGN) to quickly lift and shift (rehost) a large number of servers from physical, virtual, or cloud infrastructure to AWS.

Concept note-3: -Rehost. This strategy is also known as lift and shift. Using this strategy, you move your applications from your source environment to the AWS Cloud without making any changes to the application. For example, you migrate your application stack from on-premises to the AWS Cloud.

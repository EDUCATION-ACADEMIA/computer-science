How does Power qualify?

(A) High flexibility in use It can be used for small or large jobs.

(B) It is an operating system that supports network applications. especially TCP/IP networks

(C) ** able to perform complex tasks well and have competence in various fields more than other operating systems

(D) Can have multiple users at a time and multitasking simultaneously

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Multitasking describes a computer operating system that is capable of running multiple applications at the same time. Multithreading describes an operating system that can support two or more CPUs.

Concept note-2: -Multitasking involves overlapping and interleaving the execution of several programs. This is often achieved by capitalizing on the difference between a computer’s rapid processing capacity and the slower rates of its input/output devices.

Concept note-3: -The education needed to be an operating systems specialist is normally a bachelor’s degree. Operating systems specialists usually study business, computer science or computer information systems. 62% of operating systems specialists hold a bachelor’s degree and 22% hold a associate degree.

Concept note-4: -By cutting down processing time needed to complete a certain task, not only power consumption could be reduced, but it could also lead to greater system performance without the need to redevelop the current system.

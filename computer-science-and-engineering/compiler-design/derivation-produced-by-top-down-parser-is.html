[SOURCES]
Derivation produced by top-down parser is

(A) ** leftmost

(B) rightmost

(C) either left or right

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A top-down parsing technique parses the input by starts constructing a parse tree from the root node moving down to the leaf nodes. A top-down parser uses the leftmost derivation to generate the string.

Concept note-2: -Top-Down Parsers uses leftmost derivation to construct a parse tree.

Concept note-3: -The reason that top-down parsing follow the left-most derivation for an input string and not the right-most derivation is that the input string is scanned by the parser from left to right, one symbol/token at a time.

Concept note-4: -Top-down parsing (LL) A top down parser is called LL parser because it parses the input from Left to right, and constructs a Leftmost derivation of the sentence.

Concept note-5: -If the sentential form of an input is scanned and replaced from left to right, it is called left-most derivation. The sentential form derived by the left-most derivation is called the left-sentential form.

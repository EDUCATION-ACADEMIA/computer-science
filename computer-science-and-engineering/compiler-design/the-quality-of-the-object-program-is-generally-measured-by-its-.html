The quality of the object program is generally measured by its ____

(A) Cost

(B) Time

(C) ** Size or Its running time

(D) Code Optimization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Size-oriented metrics are a direct measure of software and the development process. Effort (time), money spent, KLOC (thousands of lines of code), pages of documentation written, errors, and individuals on the project are examples of these measures.

Concept note-2: -The three aspects of software quality are functional quality, structural quality, and process quality.

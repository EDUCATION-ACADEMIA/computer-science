How many swaps needed to sort the following numbers using a selection sort.5, 1, 12, -5, 16, 2, 12, 14

(A) 8

(B) 6

(C) 7

(D) ** 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Expert-Verified Answer Total 18 swapping required to get the bubble sort.

Concept note-2: -Total 10 swaps are required to sort the array.

Concept note-3: -Selection sort performs (at most) n – 1 swaps between data elements, while the bubble sort swaps n * (n – 1) / 2 elements in the worst case (when the list is sorted in reverse order).

Concept note-4: -Explanation: Even though the first two elements are already sorted, bubble sort needs 4 iterations to sort the given array.

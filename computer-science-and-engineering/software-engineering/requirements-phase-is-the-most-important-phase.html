SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

[SOURCES]
Requirements phase is the most important phase

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The requirements phase is where you decide upon the foundations of your software. It tells your development team what they need to be doing and without this, they would be unable to do their jobs at all.

Concept note-2: -The testing phase of the SDLC is one of the most important. It is impossible to deliver quality software without testing.

Concept note-3: -All SDLC stages should be valued equally. However, many software development experts suggest that the requirement collection and analysis stage is the most important aspect of SDLC. This is when the project team begins to understand what the stakeholders expect from the project.

Concept note-4: -The purpose of the Requirements Analysis Phase is to transform the needs and high-level requirements specified in earlier phases into unambiguous (measurable and testable), traceable, complete, consistent, and stakeholder-approved requirements.

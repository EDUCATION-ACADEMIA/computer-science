The ISO 9000 series of standards is a program that can be used for external quality assurance purposes.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ISO 9000 series of standards is a program that can be used for external quality assurance purposes. Explanation: The ISO 9000 series of standards is a set of documents.

Concept note-2: -ISO 9000 is defined as a set of international standards on quality management and quality assurance developed to help companies effectively document the quality system elements needed to maintain an efficient quality system. They are not specific to any one industry and can be applied to organizations of any size.

Concept note-3: -ISO 9000 is devoted to quality management. It is designed to help companies and other organizations ensure that the products and services they create and the processes they use to create them meet a high standard of quality and integrity.

Concept note-4: -ISO 9000 deals with the fundamentals of QMS, including the seven quality management principles that underlie the family of standards. ISO 9001 deals with the requirements that organizations wishing to meet the standard must fulfill. ISO 9002 is a model for quality assurance in production and installation.

Concept note-5: -Types of ISO 9000 Quality Standards The types of industries to which the various ISO standards apply are as follows. ISO 9001: This standard applies to the organizations engaged in design, development, production, and servicing of goods. This is the standard that applies to most software development organizations.

____ is the ability of a computer to perform tasks that require human intelligence

(A) skill

(B) ** AI

(C) Data

(D) Application

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Artificial intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer or a robot controlled by a computer to do tasks that are usually done by humans because they require human intelligence and discernment.

Concept note-2: -[1.1] A: False. Computers with AI use machine intelligence to make decisions.

Concept note-3: -Artificial intelligence (AI) makes it possible for machines to learn from experience, adjust to new inputs and perform human-like tasks. Most AI examples that you hear about today – from chess-playing computers to self-driving cars – rely heavily on deep learning and natural language processing.

Concept note-4: -Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.

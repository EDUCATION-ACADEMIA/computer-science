Which of the following is a building block of UML?

(A) Things

(B) Relationships

(C) Diagrams

(D) ** All of the mentioned

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UML is composed of three main building blocks, i.e., things, relationships, and diagrams.

Concept note-2: -(1) Things: These are known to be the most important when it comes to the building blocks of UML. They can be further divided into (a) Structural, (b) Behavioral, (c) Grouping and (d) Annotational.

Concept note-3: -The following diagram elements are classifiers in UML models: Actors. Artifacts. Classes.

Concept note-4: -UML was set up as a standardized model to describe an object-oriented programming approach. Since classes are the building block of objects, class diagrams are the building blocks of UML.

Concept note-5: -Which of the following is a building block of UML? Explanation: All are the building blocks of UML which are further sub-categorized. Explanation: Structural things are mostly static parts of a model, representing elements that are either conceptual or physical.

The three software engineers who create a standard set of diagramming techniques known as UML are:

(A) ** Grady Booch

(B) Peter Anderson

(C) ** Ivar Jacobson

(D) ** James Rumbaugh

(E) Karl Jung

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the Unified Modelling Language (UML) is largely the product of three well known software engineers, -Grady Booch, Ivar Jacobson and James Rumbaugh.

Concept note-2: -Grady Booch, Ivar Jacobson, and James Rumbaugh created the Unified Modeling Language in 1995 while working at Rational software. In 1997, the Object Management Group adopted UML as a standard for its members, which includes the likes of Hewlett-Packard, IBM, and Apple Computer.

Concept note-3: -UML, which stands for Unified Modeling Language, is a way to visually represent the architecture, design, and implementation of complex software systems.

Mention any two indirect measures of product.

(A) Quality

(B) Efficiency

(C) Accuracy

(D) ** Both A and B

(E) Both B and C

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indirect measures examine the quality of the software product itself (e.g. functionality, complexity, efficiency, reliability, maintainability).

Concept note-2: -➡ What is an indirect measure, and why are such measures common in software metrics work? An indirect measure is the product includes functionality, quality, complexity, efficiency, reliability, maintainability, and many other quality attributes.

Concept note-3: -Indirect measures include functionality, quality, complexity, efficiency, reliability, and maintainability.

Concept note-4: -Direct measures include software processes like cost and effort applied and products like lines of code produced, execution speed, and other defects that have been reported. Indirect measures include products like functionality, quality, complexity, reliability, maintainability, and many more.

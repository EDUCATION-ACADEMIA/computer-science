What does the study of an existing system referto?

(A) Details of ER diagram

(B) Feasibility Study

(C) ** System Analysis

(D) System Planning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: c) System Analysis. Explanation: The study of an existing system refers to system analysis.

Concept note-2: -An existing software system is any software application that is currently in use. It includes everything from newly released software to those that have existed for years.

Concept note-3: -Systems analysis is the process by which an individual (s) studies a system such that an information system can be analyzed, modeled, and a logical alternative can be chosen. Systems analysis projects are initiated for three reasons: problems, opportunities, and directives.

Concept note-4: -Make a list of the outputs (statements, reports) containing information. Get the contents of the reports approved by the head of the department. Analyze the requirements of the information and reports from the utility point of view. Compare the costs of the old and the new system, and benefits offered.

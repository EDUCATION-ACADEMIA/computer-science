COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

[SOURCES]
Select the incorrect statement:“Software engineers should

(A) not knowingly accept work that is outside your competence.”

(B) not use your technical skills to misuse other people’s computers.”

(C) ** be dependent on their colleagues.”

(D) maintain integrity and independence in their professional judgment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1 Answer. Correct option is (c) be dependent on their colleagues.”

Concept note-2: -Explanation: Software engineers shall act in a manner that is in the best interests of their client and employer consistent with the public interest and shall ensure that their products and related modifications meet the highest professional standards possible.

Concept note-3: -Which of these is incorrect ? Explanation: Software engineering is a vast sub domain which comes under computer science which is main domain.

Concept note-4: -A software professional and engineer can work as independent contractors or full-time employees. Similar companies hire for these roles, ranging from technology startups to AI companies and cloud service providers.

Concept note-5: -In reality, while software engineers do spend quite a bit of time at their computers, they can have a lot of social interaction at work, both among other engineers as well as other employees at the company.

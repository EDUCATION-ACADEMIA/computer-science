AN ACT PROVIDING THE FRAMEWORK ANDSUPPORT SYSTEM FOR THE OWNERSHIP, MANAGEMENT, USE, AND COMMERCIALIZATION OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY GENERATED FROM RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT FUNDED BY GOVERNMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

(A) REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10155

(B) REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11155

(C) REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11055

(D) ** REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10055

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Philippine Technology Transfer Act of 2009 (Republic No. 10055). Title abbreviation. An Act Providing the Framework and Support System for the Ownership, Management, Use, and Commercialization of Intellectual Property Generated from Research and Development funded by Government and for other purposes.

Concept note-2: -Objective.-This Act aims to promote and facilitate the transfer, dissemination, and effective use, management, and commercialization of intellectual property, technology and knowledge resulting from R&D funded by the government for the benefit of national economy and taxpayers.

Concept note-3: -– This Act shall be known as the “Data Privacy Act of 2012″. Section 2. Declaration of Policy. – It is the policy of the State to protect the fundamental human right of privacy, of communication while ensuring free flow of information to promote innovation and growth.

Concept note-4: -2067. AN ACT TO INTEGRATE, COORDINATE, AND INTENSIFY SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT AND TO FOSTER INVENTION; TO PROVIDE FUNDS THEREFOR; AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

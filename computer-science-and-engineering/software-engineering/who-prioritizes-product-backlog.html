Who prioritizes product backlog?

(A) ** Product owner

(B) Scrum team

(C) Scrum master

(D) Agent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The product owner prioritizes the backlog at the start of the project, but doesnâ€™t adjust it as feedback rolls in from developers and stakeholders. The team limits items on the backlog to those that are customer-facing.

Concept note-2: -The product owner shows up at the sprint planning meeting with the prioritized agile product backlog and describes the top items to the team. The team then determines which items they can complete during the coming sprint.

Concept note-3: -Product backlog management falls to the product manager, who has the key responsibility to create, prioritize, and maintain it.

Concept note-4: -That means a change in priority may lead to a massive change to the team structure, work products, as well as the end result. It is, therefore, crucial for scrum teams to be successful and that only one person sets the priority. That person is the product owner.

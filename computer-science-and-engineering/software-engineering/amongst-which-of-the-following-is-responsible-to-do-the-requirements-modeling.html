COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

[SOURCES]
Amongst which of the following is responsible to do the requirements modeling?

(A) ** Software engineer or an Analyst

(B) System Engineer

(C) Network administration

(D) None of the mentioned above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Amongst which of the following is responsible to do the requirements modeling? Explanation: A software engineer or an analyst creates the model based on requirements gathered from the client. To validate software requirements, an analyst examines them from a number of different points of view.

Concept note-2: -Requirements modeling is the process used in software development projects where requirements and solutions constantly evolve through collaborative efforts and teamwork. By using this method of cross-functional and self-organizing teams, you can ensure that your team meets the exact needs of the stakeholders.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: Requirement Elicitation, Requirement Analysis, Requirement Documentation and Requirement Review are the four crucial process steps of requirement engineering.

Concept note-4: -Software requirements establish the agreement between your team and the customer on what the application is supposed to do. Without a description of what features will be included and details on how the features will work, the users of the software can’t determine if the software will meet their needs.

Concept note-5: -Requirements engineers are responsible for the analysis, documentation, coordination and management of requirements regarding new software projects. They identify and understand the customer’s project-specific demands and document them in a clear and binding way.

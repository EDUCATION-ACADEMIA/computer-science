(A) ** WordPress

(B) Global Group

(C) Library

(D) XML

(E) Scroll-Jacking

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -WordPress.org is the best blogging platform for beginners. It powers over 43% of websites on the internet. It’s a free, open-source blogging platform allowing you to build a stunning website within minutes. It is an excellent option if you want things done independently.

Concept note-2: -WordPress (www.wordpress.org) WordPress.org is the king of free blogging sites. It is a free platform, but you need to build the site mostly by yourself afterward. You also have to host the software yourself.

Concept note-3: -1. WordPress.org. WordPress.org is the world’s most popular blogging software. Started in 2003, WordPress now powers more than 43% of all websites on the internet.

Concept note-4: -WordPress is free, as are many of its add-ons. Still, at the very least you’ll need to spend money on both hosting and a domain. Fortunately, entry-level hosting services are common and priced very affordably. Some of the top WordPress hosting services offer shared plans starting at less than $5 per month.

Concept note-5: -WordPress is the most popular blogging platform in the world. It’s free to use and the potential to create is boundless. You can blog for free at WordPress.com. The company hosts your site and gives you a subdomain.

Which of the following gate will give a 0 when both of its inputs are 1?

(A) AND

(B) OR

(C) ** NAND

(D) EXOR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From the above shown logic tables, we can say that for NAND and NOR gate, we will get the output ‘0’ when both the inputs are 1.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: The NAND gate gives 0 as the output when both of its inputs are 1 or any one of the input is 1.

Concept note-3: -The output of a NAND gate is 0.

Concept note-4: -When the inputs are 1 and 0, the output is zero.

Concept note-5: -AND gate-output is 1 if BOTH inputs are 1. OR gate-output is 1 if AT LEAST one input is 1. XOR gate-output is 1 if ONLY one input is 1. NAND gate-output is 1 if AT LEAST one input is 0.

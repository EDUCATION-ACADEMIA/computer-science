Evaluate your system and make recommendations for fixing any errors found

(A) Search Tools

(B) ** Diagnostic Programs

(C) Disk Management Programs

(D) Uninstall utilities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Computer diagnostic tools can be used scan your computer’s hard drive, check your ram for errors, check cpu temp and give you information about any other devices connected to your computer. These tools are made to find problems that may be disrupting your computer’s normal performance.

Concept note-2: -Some of the problems that you face every day on your computer can easily be fixed if you can adequately diagnose them. Computer problems can be hardware of software-related. Through a well-executed diagnostic procedure, you will be able to pinpoint the exact problem that is impairing your computer’s functionality.

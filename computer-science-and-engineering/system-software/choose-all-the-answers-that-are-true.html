SYSTEM SOFTWARE

[SOURCES]
Choose all the answers that are TRUE.

(A) ** Access control means that the operating system specifically asks your permission before any software can be installed

(B) Software control means that the operating system specifically asks your permission before any software can be installed.

(C) ** The purpose of access control is to try to make it impossible for malware to be secretly installed on your computer without your knowledge.

(D) The purpose of access control is to try to make it possible for malware to be secretly installed on your computer without your knowledge.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As part of an organization’s security and data protection program, it should have a plan in place that, at minimum, mitigates risk to systems and networks arising from four common types of malicious software: trojans, spyware, viruses, and ransomware.

Concept note-2: -True. Malicious software that is installed secretly on your computer by other applications and that tracks your online movements is called spyware. True. In today’s legal environment, business managers who violate the law through the misuse of information systems are still unlikely to be fully prosecuted.

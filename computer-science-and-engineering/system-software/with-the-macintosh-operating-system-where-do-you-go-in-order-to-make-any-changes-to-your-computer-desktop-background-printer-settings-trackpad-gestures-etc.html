With the Macintosh Operating System where do you go in order to make any changes to your computer (desktop background, printer settings, trackpad gestures, etc ____ )?

(A) ** System Preferences

(B) Finder

(C) Mission Control

(D) Launchpad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To change these settings, choose Apple menu > System Settings, then click Trackpad in the sidebar. (You may need to scroll down.)

Concept note-2: -Mouse gestures For more information about these gestures, choose Apple menu > System Preferences, then click Mouse. There you can turn a gesture off, change the type of gesture, and learn which gestures work with your Mac.

Concept note-3: -On your Mac, use the Advanced pane in Privacy & Security settings to adjust the security settings of your Mac. To change these settings, choose Apple menu > System settings, click Privacy & Security in the sidebar, then click Advanced on the right. (You may need to scroll down.)

Concept note-4: -The System Preferences application (basically, the settings on your Mac) is found in your Applications folder. It is also available from the Apple menu at the top-left of the screen (click the Apple logo).

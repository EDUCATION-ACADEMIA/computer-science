Example of proprietary source mobile operating system

(A) ** iOS

(B) ** Windows Mobile

(C) Android

(D) Tizen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Foundations Of Computing As each of the computer systems requires one of the operating systems for running the associated programs. This is the correct option because windows is a proprietary OS.

Concept note-2: -The most well-known mobile OSs are Android, iOS, Windows phone OS, and Symbian. The market share ratios of those OSs are Android 47.51%, iOS 41.97%, Symbian 3.31%, and Windows phone OS 2.57%. There are some other mobile OSs that are less used (BlackBerry, Samsung, etc.) [46].

Concept note-3: -Apple iOS is a proprietary OS with a fixed interface, whereas Android is an open source OS that offers more flexibility and customization. Android’s global market share in 2022 was 71.85%, according to a Statcounter report. Apple iOS’ global market share was 27.5%.

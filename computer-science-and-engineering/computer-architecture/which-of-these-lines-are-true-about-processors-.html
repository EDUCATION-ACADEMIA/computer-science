Which of these lines are true about processors !

(A) ** A PROCESSOR IS THE MAIN PART OF A PC OR MOBILE PHONE

(B) ** IF A PROCESSOR GETS HOT, THEN IT SLOWS DOWNIF A PROCESSOR GETS HOT, THEN IT SLOWS DOWN

(C) 1 GHz = 10, 000MHz1 GHz = 10, 000MHz

(D) GHz IN PROCESSORS O NOT MATTER.GHz IN PROCESSORS O NOT MATTER.

(E) ** 1GHz = 1, 000MHz1GHz = 1, 000MHz

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One gigahertz is equal to 1, 000 megahertz (MHz) or 1, 000, 000, 000 Hz. Gigahertz is often used to measure central processing unit (CPU) clock speed. In general, higher CPU clock speeds indicate faster computers.

Concept note-2: -The original Athlon (now called Athlon Classic) was the first seventh-generation x86 processor and the first desktop processor to reach speeds of one gigahertz (GHz). It made its debut as AMD’s high-end processor brand on June 23, 1999.

Concept note-3: -1. GHz is short form of gigahertz, is a unit of A.C(alternating current ) or electromagnetic (EM) wave frequencies measurement equal to 1, 000, 000, 000 Hz. 1 GHz=1000000000 Hz means frequency 1000000000 times min to max in a second.

Concept note-4: -If you’re wondering how to check your clock speed, click the Start menu (or click the Windows key) and type “System Information.” Your CPU’s model name and clock speed will be listed under “Processor.”

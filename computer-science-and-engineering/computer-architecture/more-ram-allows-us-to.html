COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE

[SOURCES]
More RAM allows us to

(A) Crash our PC

(B) Install programs

(C) ** Run more programs at one time

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Generally, the more space you have on the RAM, the more space your computer has to place multiple files and programs. This allows you to access numerous programs simultaneously and gives them room to open faster. However, your computer resorts to using virtual memory when you don’t have enough RAM space.

Concept note-2: -Understanding RAM Generally, the more RAM your computer has, the larger the digital countertop you have to work on and the faster your programs will run. If your computer is running slowly due to a lack of RAM, you might be tempted to increase virtual memory because it is less expensive.

Concept note-3: -Important RAM Specs The higher the capacity, the more data can be stored by applications. At higher capacities, more applications can run simultaneously, and games can store larger amounts of temporary data.

Concept note-4: -Most users need about 8 GB of RAM, but to use several apps at once, you might need 16 GB or more. If you don’t have enough RAM, your computer will run slowly and apps will lag. VRAM is located on your graphics card and stores temporary graphical data from apps and games.

What color can a pull-over be that you are wearing in class? Select all that apply.

(A) Black

(B) ** Navy

(C) ** White

(D) ** Teal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Teal is a deep blue-green color, named for the colored area around the eye of the common teal bird. Its hex code is #008080. Teal combines the calming properties of blue with the renewal qualities of green.

Concept note-2: -Blue’s complementary color is orange, and teal works beautifully with earthy terracotta or warming paprika. Green’s complementary hue is red, so just as leafy shades partner perfectly with pink, teal also sits pretty with blush.

Concept note-3: -Information about Teal Green / #006D5B In a RGB color space (made from three colored lights for red, green, and blue), hex #006D5B is made of 0% red, 42.7% green and 35.7% blue.

To reverse an image either horizontally or vertically.

(A) Distort

(B) Skew

(C) ** Flip

(D) Rotate

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the Arrange menu, point to Rotate or Flip. Do one of the following: To flip an object horizontally, click Flip Horizontal. To flip an object vertically, click Flip Vertical.

Concept note-2: -Right click on that object, and select ‘Flip Horizontal’ or ‘Flip Vertical’.

Concept note-3: -You can access the horizontal flip command from the image menubar through Image → Transform → Flip Horizontally. You can access the vertical flip command from the image menubar through Image → Transform → Flip Vertically.

Concept note-4: -Tap Edit in the bottom-left to reveal the available editing tools. Select Rotate from the available options. You’ll see a time icon in the bottom. Tap this icon to flip your photo.

Concept note-5: -Horizontal Flip Vs. Horizontal Flip flips an image along the y-axis; Vertical Flip flips an image along the x-axis.

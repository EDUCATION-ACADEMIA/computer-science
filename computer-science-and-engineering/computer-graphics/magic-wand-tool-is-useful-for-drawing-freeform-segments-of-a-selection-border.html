Magic wand tool is useful for drawing freeform segments of a selection border.

(A) T

(B) ** F

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Lasso tool is useful for drawing freeform segments of a selection border.

Concept note-2: -What does the Magic Wand tool do? The Magic Wand tool automatically selects an object or area of your image, using machine learning to discern what you might be trying to select. If you click on the sky, it selects the sky; click on a person’s face, and you have the entire face selected in just seconds.

Concept note-3: -The Lasso tool is useful for drawing freeform segments of a selection border. Select the Lasso tool, and set feathering and anti-aliasing in the options bar.

Concept note-4: -The Magic Wand tool is a selection tool. It allows you to quickly select areas of your images and make independent edits to it. It’s most used often to select solid backgrounds and color areas.

Concept note-5: -The Lasso tool is for making free-form selections. Select the Lasso tool in the Tools panel. Click and drag to make a free-form selection. When you’re back near the beginning, release your finger from the mouse/trackpad to close the selection.

____ is used for detecting mouse motion.

(A) Optical Sensor

(B) Rollers on the bottom of mouse

(C) ** Both a and b

(D) Sensor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: c Explanation: Rollers and optical sensors are used to record the amount and direction of movement.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Trackball. A trackball is an input device used to enter motion data into computers or other electronic devices. Trackballs designed for computers usually serve as mouse replacements and are essentially used to move the cursor on the screen.

When you are creating graphical images, which technique can you use to control and manipulate elements individually?

(A) Opacity

(B) ** Layers

(C) Shapes

(D) Transparency

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nanotechnology is the study and manipulation of individual atoms and molecules.

Concept note-2: -Another method of creating graphics on a computer is called raster graphics, whereby the image is created using colored dots, called pixels. This method is often used for images with lots of different colors, such as photographs and collages.

Concept note-3: -The Rectangular Marquee tool is used to make rectangular and square selections. Click and drag to make a rectangular selection. If you want to constrain the selection to be a square, press the Shift key as you’re dragging. The Elliptical Marquee tool is used to make oval and circle selections.

Concept note-4: -The Magic Wand tool selects pixels within a similar color range with one click. You specify the color range, or tolerance, for the Magic Wand tool’s selection. Use the Magic Wand tool when you have an area of similar colors, like a blue sky.

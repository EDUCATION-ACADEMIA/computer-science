Cohen-Sutherland clipping is an example of ____

(A) polygon clipping

(B) text clipping

(C) ** line clipping

(D) curve clipping

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: c Explanation: It is a type of algorithm which is used for line clipping or in other words it is line clipping algorithm.

Concept note-2: -In computer graphics, the Cohen–Sutherland algorithm is an algorithm used for line clipping. The algorithm divides a two-dimensional space into 9 regions and then efficiently determines the lines and portions of lines that are visible in the central region of interest (the viewport).

Concept note-3: -In computer graphics, line clipping is the process of removing (clipping) lines or portions of lines outside an area of interest (a viewport or view volume). Typically, any part of a line which is outside of the viewing area is removed.

Concept note-4: -Other examples of line clipping algorithms are a Liang-Barsky algorithm and Cyrus-Beck algorithm.

Concept note-5: -The line clipping algorithms are: Cohen Sutherland Line Clipping Algorithm. Midpoint Subdivision Line Clipping Algorithm. Liang-Barsky Line Clipping Algorithm.

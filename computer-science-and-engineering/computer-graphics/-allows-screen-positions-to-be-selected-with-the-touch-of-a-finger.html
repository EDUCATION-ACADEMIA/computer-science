____ allows screen positions to be selected with the touch of a finger.

(A) ** Touch panels

(B) Image Scanner

(C) Light Pen

(D) Mouse

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Option A is correct, Explanation-Touch panels allows screen positions to be selected with the touch of a finger.

Concept note-2: -A touchpad or trackpad is a flat surface that can detect finger contact. It is a stationary pointing device, commonly used on laptop computers.

Concept note-3: -Answer: a Explanation: A mouse is small hand-held box used to position the screen cursor.

Concept note-4: -9. Which device contains thumbwheel, trackball and a standard mouse ball? Explanation: These 3 buttons are the Z mouse features.

Concept note-5: -Answer: b Explanation: Input devices provide positional information to the system they are often called pointing devices.

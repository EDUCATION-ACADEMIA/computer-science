The Cohen-Sutherland algorithm divides the region into ____ number of spaces.

(A) 8

(B) 6

(C) 7

(D) ** 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2. The Cohen-Sutherland algorithm divides the region into number of spaces. Explanation: The Cohen-Sutherland algorithm divides a two-dimensional space into 9 regions and then efficiently determines the lines and portions of lines that are visible. The portions are visible in the central region of interest.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: The 4-bit code of the bottom-right region amongst the nine regions divided by the Cohen-Sutherland algorithm is 0110.

Concept note-3: -Region code of (5, 1) and (25, 30) respectively in cohen sutherland line clipping algorithm is: 0000, 1010 None 1001, 1010 0001, 1010.

Concept note-4: -Cohen Sutherland uses region code to clip a portion of the line which is not present in the visible region. It divides a region into 9 columns based on (X MAX, Y MAX) and (X MIN, Y MIN).

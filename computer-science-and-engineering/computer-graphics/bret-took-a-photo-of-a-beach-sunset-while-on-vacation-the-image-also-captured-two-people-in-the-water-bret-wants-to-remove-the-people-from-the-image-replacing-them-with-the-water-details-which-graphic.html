COMPUTER GRAPHICS

[SOURCES]
Bret took a photo of a beach sunset while on vacation. The image also captured two people in the water. Bret wants to remove the people from the image, replacing them with the water details. Which graphics tool will allow Bret to copy the water details and paste them in another area on the same image?

(A) Color Replace

(B) Burn

(C) ** Clone Stamp

(D) Dodge

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Resizing an image deletes the unused portion of the image. Cropping an image keeps the unused portion of the image.

Concept note-2: -You should use a JPEG when… You want to print photos and/or artwork. At high resolution files with low compression, JPEGs are perfect for editing and then printing.

Concept note-3: -The Magic Wand tool selects pixels within a similar color range with one click. You specify the color range, or tolerance, for the Magic Wand tool’s selection. Use the Magic Wand tool when you have an area of similar colors, like a blue sky.

Concept note-4: -Definition of GRAPHICS SOFTWARE: Graphics software can edit bitmap and/or vector graphics, and can be used to design label templates. Examples of graphics software include Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, CorelDraw, Inkscape, Microsoft Paint, and Paint.Net.

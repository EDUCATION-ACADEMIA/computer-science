To capture sound from a CD, burning software is used

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CDBurnerXP-Best for All Kinds of Discs Image from Softonic. CDBurnerXP is an entirely free CD burning software that allows you to burn various disc types from your computer. This program can burn CDs and DVDs, including HD DVDs and Blu-ray discs.

Concept note-2: -When you copy music, pictures, and videos from your PC to a blank CD or DVD, it’s called “burning.” When you copy music, pictures, and videos from a CD or DVD to your PC, it’s called “ripping.” You can use Windows Media Player to do both.

Concept note-3: -Create an audio CD on Windows Media Player, click the Burn tab. Drag the songs you want to burn into the sidebar. Click “Menu” and check “Audio CD". Click Start burn.

Concept note-4: -Answer: To burn CD simply means to copy or write information onto a compact disc or CD. CD drives that are capable to writing Cds will use a laser to burn the information onto the underside of the CD and allow it to be read in CD players or CD ROM drives.

Clipart is

(A) Any image found on the www(world wide web) that look cool.

(B) Art that has been photoshopped and cropped.

(C) ** Lots of images/pictures imported into a document

(D) black & white line drawings of paper clips

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clip art refers to a graphic or a picture that you can insert in your document. It comes in different formats and styles. It is used to enhance the appearance of a document.

Concept note-2: -Clip art (also clipart, clip-art) is a type of graphic art. Pieces are pre-made images used to illustrate any medium. Today, clip art is used extensively and comes in many forms, both electronic and printed. However, most clip art today is created, distributed, and used in a digital form.

Concept note-3: -or clipart (ˈklɪpˌɑːt ) noun. a large collection of simple drawings stored in a computer from which items can be selected for incorporation into documents.

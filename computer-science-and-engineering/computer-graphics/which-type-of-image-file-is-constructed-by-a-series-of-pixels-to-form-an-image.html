Which type of image file is constructed by a series of pixels to form an image?

(A) Vector

(B) PDF

(C) ** Raster

(D) SVG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raster images are constructed by a series of pixels, or individual blocks, to form an image. JPEG, GIF, and PNG are all raster image extensions.

Concept note-2: -Popular types of raster files include JPEG, PNG, and GIF images.

Concept note-3: -A raster image is an image that is composed of a grid of pixels. Raster images are typically used for digital photos and web graphics.

Concept note-4: -raster graphics, also called bitmap graphics, a type of digital image that uses tiny rectangular pixels, or picture elements, arranged in a grid formation to represent an image.

Concept note-5: -Raster files are images built from pixels-tiny color squares that, in great quantity, can form highly detailed images such as photographs.

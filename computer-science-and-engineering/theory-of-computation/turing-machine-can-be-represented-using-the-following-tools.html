Concept note-1: -Explanation: We can represent a turing machine, graphically, tabularly and diagramatically.

Concept note-5: -Turing machines, first described by Alan Turing in Turing 1936–7, are simple abstract computational devices intended to help investigate the extent and limitations of what can be computed.

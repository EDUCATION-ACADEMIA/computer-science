Number of states require to accept string ends with 10.

(A) ** 3

(B) 2

(C) 1

(D) can’t be represented.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Number of states require to accept string ends with 10. d) can’t be represented. Explanation: This is minimal finite automata. 4.

Concept note-2: -Hence, 4 states will be required.

Concept note-3: -L=01, 001, 101, 110001, 1001, ………. The minimum length of the string is 2, the number of states that the DFA consists of for the given language is: 2+1 = 3 states.

Concept note-4: -1 Answer. Number of finfinal state(s) require to accept in minimal finite automata is ZERO.

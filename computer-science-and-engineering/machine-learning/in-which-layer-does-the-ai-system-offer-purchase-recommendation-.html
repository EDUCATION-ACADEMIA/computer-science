In which layer does the AI system offer “Purchase Recommendation “?

(A) Sensing

(B) Reasoning

(C) ** Learning

(D) Acting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A recommendation system is an artificial intelligence or AI algorithm, usually associated with machine learning, that uses Big Data to suggest or recommend additional products to consumers. These can be based on various criteria, including past purchases, search history, demographic information, and other factors.

Concept note-2: -What Are AI-Based Product Recommendations? A recent study from the research firm Orbis Research described AI-based recommendation engines as “data-filtering tools that make use of various algorithms and data to recommend the most relevant items to a particular customer.”

Concept note-3: -Deep learning applications are used in industries from automated driving to medical devices. Automated Driving: Automotive researchers are using deep learning to automatically detect objects such as stop signs and traffic lights. In addition, deep learning is used to detect pedestrians, which helps decrease accidents.

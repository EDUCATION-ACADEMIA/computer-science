COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

MACHINE LEARNING

[SOURCES]
the process of making the system able to learn.

(A) ** Training

(B) Labeling

(C) Testing

(D) Classifying

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Machine learning is the process of making systems that learn and improve by themselves, by being specifically programmed.

Concept note-2: -Training is an organized process of intervention to improve current knowledge, abilities and skills of the workforce. Training involve a number of stages. It involves Training Need Analysis (TNA), Designing of Training Program, Implementation of Training, and Evaluation of the Training.

Concept note-3: -acquisition. noun. the process of learning skills or getting knowledge.

Concept note-4: -The process of training and development is an activity that aims to improve the performance of the individuals and groups in an organisation. Organisations curate systematic procedures to educate employees on the necessary technical and management skills for their roles.

Concept note-5: -Learning type 1: auditive learning (“by listening and speaking“), Learning type 2: visual learning (“through the eyes, by watching”), • Learning type 3: haptic learning (“by touching and feeling”), • Learning type 4: learning through the intellect.

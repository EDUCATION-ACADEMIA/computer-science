Identify Currency

(A) ** Supervised Learning

(B) Unsupervised Learning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ascending font size One of the easiest ways to spot a counterfeit is to look for any anomalies in the number panel at the top left and the bottom right side of the note. An authentic currency note will display these numbers in an ascending pattern from left to right.

Concept note-2: -Detection of Fake Currency Using KNN Algorithm.

Concept note-3: -1.3 Objective The main objective of the project is to identify the fake Indian currency notes automatically using Morphological Algorithm. Although there were many methods in existence, this method was designed to overcome the drawbacks of the previous methods.

Concept note-4: -Nowadays, every country is facing the currency recognition problem due to the fake denominations in the market or illiteracy of the people in the given country, so automatic currency recognition system is important.

Some of the major benefits of a smart factory?

(A) Boosting Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) only

(B) Boosting Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) and Flexible, adaptive, and proactive production

(C) ** Boosting Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE), Flexible, adaptive, and proactive production and Real-time, on-demand visibility into performance across the production chain

(D) Reduce manpower

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among said benefits are: The smart factory is more efficient than traditional manufacturing because all processes are optimally aligned. Production costs are lower, due in part to reduced downtime and less waste. Underutilized production capacity can be identified and eliminated.

Concept note-2: -A smart factory is a digitised manufacturing facility that uses connected devices, machinery and production systems to continuously collect and share data.

Concept note-3: -Through a people-centered digital platform and powered by execution applications, EY Smart Factory embeds manufacturing excellence by arming the shopfloor with dynamic predictive data analytics, virtual reality and artificial intelligence to deliver unprecedented performance.

Concept note-4: -Smart manufacturing (SM) is a technology-driven approach that utilizes Internet-connected machinery to monitor the production process. The goal of SM is to identify opportunities for automating operations and use data analytics to improve manufacturing performance.

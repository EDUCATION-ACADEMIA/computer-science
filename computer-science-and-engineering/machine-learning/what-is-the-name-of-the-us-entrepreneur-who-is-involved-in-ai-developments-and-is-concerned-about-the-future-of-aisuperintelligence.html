What is the name of the US entrepreneur who is involved in AI developments and is concerned about the future of AI/Superintelligence?

(A) Bill Gates

(B) ** Elon Musk

(C) Sundar Pichai

(D) Mark Zuckerberg

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Billionaire Elon Musk, who recently took control of Twitter and became the new CEO of the micro-blogging platform, called the social network a “collective super-intelligence” which could be humanity’s hope against the rise of artificial intelligence.

Concept note-2: -Musk’s move is notable because in 2015 he co-founded OpenAI as a nonprofit to rival profit-minting AI tech powerhouses like Google.

Concept note-3: -Musk believes oversight for artificial intelligence is necessary, having described the technology as “potentially more dangerous than nukes.” “We need some kind of, like, regulatory authority or something overseeing A.I. development, ” he told investors yesterday. “Make sure it’s operating in the public interest.”

Concept note-4: -By his own admission, Musk no longer owns a stake in OpenAI. He is not on the board, nor does he control it in any way. (Musk did, however, have a relationship with OpenAI director Shivon Zilis, with whom he recently fathered twins.)

Concept note-5: -Musk left OpenAI’s board in 2018 and no longer holds a stake in the company. “Initially it was created as an open-source nonprofit. Now it is closed-source and for profit. I don’t have an open stake in OpenAI, nor am I on the board, nor do I control it in any way.”

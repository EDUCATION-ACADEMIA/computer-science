Artificial Intelligence is superset of ____ & ____,

(A) Machine Learning & Neural Networks

(B) ** Machine Learning & Deep Learning

(C) Deep Learning & Neural Networks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To be technically correct-AIML stands for Artificial Intelligence Modelling Language (an extensible XML-based mark-up language). AI is a superset of Machine Learning (ML), which itself is superset of deep learning. Deep learning is ML that uses neural networks.

Concept note-2: -Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence and a superset of deep learning. Artificial intelligence has evolved to create machine learning. Neural networks are used in deep learning for data representation. Also, big data is prevalent here and consists of millions of data points.

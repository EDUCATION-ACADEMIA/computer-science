(A) ** 2010

(B) 2009

(C) 2001

(D) 2008

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -The concept of deep learning has been around since the 1950s. Take a brief look at how it evolved from concept to actuality and the key people who made it happen.

Concept note-3: -AI boom: 1980-1987 Deep Learning techniques and the use of Expert System became more popular, both of which allowed computers to learn from their mistakes and make independent decisions.

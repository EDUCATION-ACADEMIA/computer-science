Concept note-3: -Linear and Logistic regression algorithms have been used to predict the weather on the basis of minimum and maximum temperatures, wind speed, humidity, precipitation and chances of rainfall.

Concept note-4: -Types of Weather Forecasting Short-range forecasts are predictions made between one and seven days before they happen. Medium-range forecasts are usually given between one week and four weeks in advance. Long-range forecasts are given between one month and a year in advance.

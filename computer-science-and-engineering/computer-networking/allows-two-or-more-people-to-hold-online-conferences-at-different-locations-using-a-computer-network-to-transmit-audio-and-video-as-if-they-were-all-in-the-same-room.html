Allows two or more people to hold online conferences at different locations using a computer network to transmit audio and video as if they were all in the same room

(A) ** Video Conferencing

(B) Video Calls

(C) Video Locations

(D) Video Line

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A teleconference is a live audio or audiovisual meeting with two or more participants. With the ability to teleconference, remote teams in an organization can collaborate and communicate, even when geographically dispersed. The process involves technology more sophisticated than a simple two-way phone connection.

Concept note-2: -Zoom, a widely popular video conferencing vendor that experienced explosive growth in 2020, became the face of pandemic video conferencing to many.

Concept note-3: -Teleconferencing is a way of bringing a group of people together from different locations without having to travel long distances. The three most common types of teleconference are conference calls (voice only), videoconferences (voice and video), and web-based conferences.

Concept note-4: -Videoconferencing example A group of people get together in a room to talk via videoconference. The people can see and hear each other because they are connected by cameras, microphones and speakers in their laptops. They use a software program called Zoom.

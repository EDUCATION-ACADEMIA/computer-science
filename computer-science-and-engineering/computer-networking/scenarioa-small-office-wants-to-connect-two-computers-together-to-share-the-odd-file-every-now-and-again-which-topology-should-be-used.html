Scenario:A small office wants to connect two computers together to share the odd file every now and again. Which topology should be used?

(A) Star

(B) ** Bus

(C) Mesh

(D) Ring

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When two or more computers are connected together so they can communicate with one another, they form a network. The largest computer network in the world in the Internet.

Concept note-2: -The wired or wireless connection of two or more computers for the purpose of sharing data and resources form a computer network.

Concept note-3: -Hub. Hubs connect multiple computer networking devices together. A hub also acts as a repeater in that it amplifies signals that deteriorate after traveling long distances over connecting cables.

Concept note-4: -A peer-to-peer network is one in which two or more PCs share files and access to devices such as printers without requiring a separate server computer or server software.

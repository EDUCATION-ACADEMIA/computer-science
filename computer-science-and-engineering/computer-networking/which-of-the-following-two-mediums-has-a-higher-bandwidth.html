Which of the following two mediums has a higher bandwidth?

(A) ** Fibre

(B) Copper

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fiber-optic bandwidth is high both because of the speed with which data can be transmitted and the distance that data can travel without attenuation. Optical fiber transmits data as pulses of light through glass wire, allowing data to travel at nearly the speed of light.

Concept note-2: -Since Fiber Optic Cable has the highest bandwidth.

Concept note-3: -Greater Bandwidth & Faster Speed: Fiber optic cable can support extremely high bandwidth and greater speeds.

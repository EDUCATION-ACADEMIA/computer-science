Your friend Maria asks you which of the following are the most secure. What do you tell her?

(A) 802.11n

(B) Infrared

(C) Cellular

(D) ** UTP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Authorization is a process by which a server determines if the client has permission to use a resource or access a file.

Concept note-2: -A. Wired networks can provide the highest data throughput rates of any of the connection types.

Concept note-3: -Address ranges to be use by private networks are: Class A: 10.0. 0.0 to 10.255. 255.255.

Concept note-4: -The addresses in the range 172.16. 0.0 through 172.31. 255.255 are all considered private, based on RFC 1918.

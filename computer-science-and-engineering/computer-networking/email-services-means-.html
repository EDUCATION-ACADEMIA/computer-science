Email services means ____

(A) ** transmission of messages from one user to another user.

(B) a group of email addresses used for mass distribution of a message

(C) that files can be uploaded and downloaded

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Email (electronic mail) is the exchange of computer-stored messages from one user to one or more recipients via the internet. Emails are a fast, inexpensive and accessible way to communicate for business or personal use.

Concept note-2: -An email client is a software program or web application* that enables users to send, receive, and store emails. Popular email clients include Outlook, Gmail, and Apple Mail. Software-and web-based email clients each have advantages and disadvantages.

Concept note-3: -Email transmission means a method of exchanging digital messages between computer users.

Concept note-4: -Emails are routed to user accounts via several computer servers. They route the message to their final destination and store them so that users can pick them up and send them once they connect to the email infrastructure. Email can be accessed through an email client or a web interface (more about these later).

Concept note-5: -SMTP. The Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP) is used for sending and receiving e-mail between e-mail clients and servers.

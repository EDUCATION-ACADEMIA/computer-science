COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

COMPUTER NETWORKING

[SOURCES]
network that covers a broad area using leased telecommunication lines

(A) LAN

(B) MAN

(C) ** WAN

(D) NET

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A wide area network (WAN) is a telecommunications network that extends over a large geographic area. Wide area networks are often established with leased telecommunication circuits.

Concept note-2: -Local area networks (LANs) are the building blocks of a WAN. A LAN consists of interconnected computers and other devices limited to a small place, such as a building, school, or office.

Concept note-3: -LAN is an acronym for Local Area Network. MAN is an acronym for Metropolitan Area Network. WAN is an acronym for Wide Area Network. Definition and Meaning. LAN is a network that usually connects a small group of computers in a given geographical area.

Concept note-4: -A WAN connection device is a network device which enables the linking of different machines that are not situated in the same location. For example, a business may use routers and other hardware to connect computers in different cities.

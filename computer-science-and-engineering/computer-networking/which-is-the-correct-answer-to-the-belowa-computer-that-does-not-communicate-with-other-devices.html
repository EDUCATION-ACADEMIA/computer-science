EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A router is a communications device that connects multiple computers or other routers together and transmits data to its correct desti-nation on the network.

Concept note-2: -If your PC is not connected to a network, it is referred to as a standalone computer.

Concept note-3: -IoT devices use embedded sensors to collect, exchange, and share data with other devices, applications, and systems, in real-time. Internet of Things is a collaboration of custom-designed technologies to interconnect internet-enabled physical devices and enable communication with each other through a wireless network.

Concept note-4: -A network interface controller (NIC, also known as a network interface card, network adapter, LAN adapter or physical network interface, and by similar terms) is a computer hardware component that connects a computer to a computer network.

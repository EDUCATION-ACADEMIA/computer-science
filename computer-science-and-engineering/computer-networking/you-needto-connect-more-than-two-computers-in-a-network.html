COMPUTER NETWORKING

[SOURCES]
You need ____ to connect more than two computers in a network.

(A) Network Interface Card

(B) Network Cable

(C) ** Network Hub

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -USB hubs enable you to attach multiple devices, such as printers, hard drives and card readers, to a single computer. However, the USB hub only makes the devices available to one computer at a time. You need to disconnect the hub from one computer and attach it to another to share a printer with more than one computer.

Concept note-2: -First connect each computer to the hub using utp cables. Take a lan cable and connect it to the PCâ€™s lan port and connect the other end of the cable to the Hub lan port. Connect all the computers using the same method. Now power on the computers and assign ip addresses to the computers.

Concept note-3: -A hub is fine for most small home networks consisting of five or less computers. If you have more than five computers, or there are heavy internet users (online gaming or streaming video, for example), a switch will reduce the network load and significantly improve network response.

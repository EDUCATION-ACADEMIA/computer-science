Which pair below are most alike

(A) Router and Modem

(B) Switch & Modem

(C) Router and Hub

(D) ** Hub & Switch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The key difference between hubs, switches and bridges is that hubs operate at Layer 1 of the OSI model, while bridges and switches work with MAC addresses at Layer 2.

Concept note-2: -Hub is a device that is used to connect more than one computer together in a network. It is a physical layer networking device. It contains multiple ports.

Concept note-3: -There are two types of network hubs: active and passive.

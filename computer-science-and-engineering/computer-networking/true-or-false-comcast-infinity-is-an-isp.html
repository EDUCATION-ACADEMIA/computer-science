True or False-Comcast Infinity is an ISP.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Comcast is an example of an ISP. Cable Internet access is faster than conventional dial-up Internet access.

Concept note-2: -Comcast’s Xfinity is both the biggest cable internet service in the U.S. and the most-used broadband provider in the country outright, with more than 31 million residential customers by the second quarter of 2021. That’s more than 10 million more than its total broadband customer base 10 years earlier.

Concept note-3: -Comcast is the parent company, and Xfinity is Comcast’s brand for TV, internet, and home phone services.

Concept note-4: -Philadelphia-based Comcast Cable Communications is a telecommunications company and division of Comcast Corporation. Comcast launched the Xfinity brand in 2010 and is the largest internet service provider in the U.S. The brand offers internet, TV, home security and phone services.

Your home computer is having problems accessing the Internet. You suspect that your Internet router’s DHCP service is not functioning, so you check your computer’s IP address. Which address indicates that your router’s DHCP service is NOT functioning?

(A) ** 169.254.1.15

(B) 172.16.1.15

(C) 192.168.1.15

(D) 10.19.1.15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you see a 169.254. x.x address, it means that the DHCP server is not reachable.

Concept note-2: -1. When the DHCP server is unavailable for a network, how will PCs attempt to configure their NICs? A. PCs will attempt to use the last address assigned to them.

Concept note-3: -The Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA) has reserved 169.254. 0.0-169.254. 255.255 for Automatic Private IP Addressing. As a result, APIPA provides an address that is guaranteed not to conflict with routable addresses.

UTP is a cable type normally associated with?

(A) ** Ethernet

(B) Fiber-optics

(C) Last-mile

(D) Coaxial bus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UTP cables are commonly used as telephone wired and ethernet cables in telephone connections and LAN (Local Area Networks). It is used to transfer audio signals and electronic data. These are also used in horizontal and backbone cabling subsystems.

Concept note-2: -Unshielded twisted pair (UTP) is a ubiquitous type of copper cabling used in telephone wiring and local area networks (LANs).

Concept note-3: -The most common structure is the Twisted Pair cable. Two wires inside the cable are twisted together. Twisted Pairs is the industry standard cable. They have the best results in terms of maximum length and speed drops.

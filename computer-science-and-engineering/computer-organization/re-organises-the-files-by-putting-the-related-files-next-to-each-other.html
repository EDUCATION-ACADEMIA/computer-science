EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defragmentation software reorganises the data on the hard drive and puts the fragmented files back together. It also moves files to collect all the free space together. This can help to prevent further fragmentation.

Concept note-2: -Utility software helps users configure, analyse, optimise and maintain a computer. This software normally consists of small programs which are thought of as part of the operating system (OS) as they often come built in with the OS.

Concept note-3: -Disk Defragmenter is a Windows utility program that located and eliminates unnecessary fragments and rearranges files and unused to disk space to optimise operations.

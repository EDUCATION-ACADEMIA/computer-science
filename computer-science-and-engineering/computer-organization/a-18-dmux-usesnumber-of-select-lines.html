COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

COMPUTER ORGANIZATION

[SOURCES]
A 1:8 DMUX uses ____ (number) of select lines.

(A) 2

(B) ** 3

(C) 4

(D) 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A 1 to 8 demultiplexer consists of one input line, 8 output lines and 3 select lines.

Concept note-2: -To implement the 1×8 de-multiplexer, we need two 1×4 de-multiplexer and one 1×2 de-multiplexer. The 1×4 multiplexer has 2 selection lines, 4 outputs, and 1 input. The 1×2 de-multiplexer has only 1 selection line. For getting 8 data outputs, we need two 1×4 de-multiplexer.

Concept note-3: -We know that 8x1 Multiplexer has 8 data inputs, 3 selection lines and one output.

Concept note-4: -A 1-to-4 demultiplexer has a single input (D), two selection lines (S1 and S0) and four outputs (Y0 to Y3).

Concept note-5: -The 8×1 multiplexer has 3 selection lines, 4 inputs, and 1 output. The 2×1 multiplexer has only 1 selection line.

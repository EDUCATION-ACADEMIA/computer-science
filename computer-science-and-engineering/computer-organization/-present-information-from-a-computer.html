COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

COMPUTER ORGANIZATION

[SOURCES]
____ present information from a computer.

(A) Memory unit

(B) Input Unit

(C) ** Output Unit

(D) CPU

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An output device allows data to be transmitted by the computer in a human-friendly form, for example, sound being played through a speaker. Monitor-The main output device of a computer. It forms images by converting electrical energy into light in the form of tiny dots on the screen called pixels.

Concept note-2: -What Does Output Device Mean? An output device is any hardware device used to send data from a computer to another device or user. Usually, most output peripherals are meant for human use, so they receive the processed data from the computer and transform it in the form of audio, video, or physical reproductions.

Concept note-3: -The output is how the computer presents the results of the process, such as text on a screen, printed materials, or sound from a speaker.

Concept note-4: -Output unit refers to the output devices that are used to communicate data from computers to users. These include hardware that transmits data from computers in a form that is understandable by humans. Examples of output units are microphones, monitors, printers, headphones, etc.

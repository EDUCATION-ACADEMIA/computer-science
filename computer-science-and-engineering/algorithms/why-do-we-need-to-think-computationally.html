Why do we need to think computationally?

(A) To help us program

(B) ** To help us solve complex problems more easily

(C) To help us to think like a computer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Computers work programmatically, following a set number of prescribed actions to solve complex problems. In the same way, computational thinking allows kids to break down complex problems of their own into more manageable steps. That way, they can better understand the problem at hand and solve it themselves!

Concept note-2: -Before you can think about programming a computer, you need to work out exactly what it is you want to tell the computer to do. Thinking through problems this way is Computational Thinking. Computational Thinking allows us to take complex problems, understand what the problem is, and develop solutions.

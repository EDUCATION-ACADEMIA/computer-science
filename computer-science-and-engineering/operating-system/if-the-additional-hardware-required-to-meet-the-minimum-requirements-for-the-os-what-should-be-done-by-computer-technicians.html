If the additional hardware required to meet the minimum requirements for the OS, what should be done by computer technicians?

(A) Buying new hardware is compatible with OS

(B) ** conduct a cost analysis to determine the best course of action

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hardware requirements These requirements include the minimum processor speed, memory, and disk space required to install Windows. In almost all cases, you will want to make sure that your hardware exceeds these requirements to provide adequate performance for the services and applications running on the server.

Concept note-2: -If your computer does not meet the minimum system requirements, the program you are attempting to install will not run and may not even install. If your computer meets the minimum requirements but does not meet the suggested requirements, the program is going to work, but it may be slow.

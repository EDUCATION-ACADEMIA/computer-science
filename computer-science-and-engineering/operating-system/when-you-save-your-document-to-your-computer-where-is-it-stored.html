When you save your document to your computer, where is it stored?

(A) in the cloud

(B) in a folder

(C) on the Internet

(D) ** on a hard drive

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By default, Microsoft 365 programs save files in the default working folder.

Concept note-2: -Most computers will automatically save your data to the hard drive, usually known as the C drive. This is the most common place to store files.

Concept note-3: -Hard disk stores information in the form of magnetic fields. Data is stored digitally in the form of tiny magnetized regions on the platter where each region represents a bit.

Concept note-4: -A computer hard drive (or a hard disk or HDD) is one kind of technology that stores the operating system, applications, and data files such a documents, pictures and music that your computer uses.

Concept note-5: -A hard drive is the hardware component that stores all of your digital content. Your documents, pictures, music, videos, programs, application preferences, and operating system represent digital content stored on a hard drive.

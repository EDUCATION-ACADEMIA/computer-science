CLOUD COMPUTING

____ feature allows you to optimize your system and capture all possible transactions.

(A) scalability

(B) reliability

(C) ** elasticity

(D) None of the mentioned

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -36 You utilize feature permits you to optimize your system and capture all possible transactions. Ans. Elasticity. You have the ability to modify the resources as required.

Concept note-2: -Regarding cloud computing, scalability and elasticity are two important concepts you need to understand. Scalability is the ability of a system or network to handle increased load or usage. At the same time, elasticity is the ability to automatically expand and contract resources to meet demand.

Concept note-3: -This is called resilient architecture. Elasticity and Scalability on the cloud is the ability for resources being used to be configured to scale. Additionally, when resources are no longer needed, elasticity and scalability provide the ability for those resources to be released.

Concept note-4: -Cloud Elasticity is the property of a cloud to grow or shrink capacity for CPU, memory, and storage resources to adapt to the changing demands of an organization.

CLOUD COMPUTING

[SOURCES]
Which among the following service is refered to edge computing

(A) CloudWave

(B) ** CloudFront

(C) CloudSpot

(D) CloudWatch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Amazon CloudFront is referred to as a content delivery network (CDN), and sometimes called edge computing.

Concept note-2: -AWS CloudFront is the content delivery service with which you can deliver content to destinations quickly and securely. It uses a global edge network of edge locations and AWS regions to deliver content with low latency.

Concept note-3: -Amazon CloudFront is a web service that speeds up distribution of your static and dynamic web content, such as .html, .css, .js, and image files, to your users. CloudFront delivers your content through a worldwide network of data centers called edge locations.

Concept note-4: -Amazon CloudFront offers programmable and secure edge CDN computing capabilities through CloudFront Functions and AWS LambdaEdge.

Concept note-5: -AWS CloudFront is a globally-distributed network offered by Amazon Web Services, which securely transfers content such as software, SDKs, videos, etc., to the clients, with high transfer speed.

CLOUD COMPUTING

[SOURCES]
What is a disadvantage of cloud storage?

(A) Files can be modified by multiple users and kept updated.

(B) Cloud storage is only for teachers.

(C) ** Can only be accessed where there is an internet connection.

(D) Files can be accessed on multiple devices.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Any confidential information about the business can be exchanged with a third-party cloud computing service provider. Hackers could exploit this knowledge. Downtime: That’s because your cloud provider may face power failure, poor access to the internet, maintenance of services, etc.

Concept note-2: -Downtime is considered as one of the biggest potential downsides of using Cloud Computing. The cloud providers may sometimes face technical outages that can happen due to various reasons, such as loss of power, low Internet connectivity, data centers going out of service for maintenance, etc.

Concept note-3: -What are the disadvantages of cloud storage?-Requires a reliable internet connection.-Cloud storage won’t work as well with low-speed connections.-Web-based apps, large documents and images require a lot of bandwidth.

Concept note-4: -Better collaboration. Cloud storage enables you to make data available anywhere you are, anytime you need it. Instead of being tied to a location or specific device, people can access data from anywhere in the world from any device-as long as they have an internet connection.

Concept note-5: -Improper handing can cause trouble: You must need your user-id and password safe to protect your data as if someone knows or even guess your credentials, it may result in loss of data. Use complex passwords and try to avoid storage them in your personal storage devices such as pen drive and HDD.

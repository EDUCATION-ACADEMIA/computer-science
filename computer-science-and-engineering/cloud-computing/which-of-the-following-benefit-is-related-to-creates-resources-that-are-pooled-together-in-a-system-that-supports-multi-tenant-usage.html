Concept note-2: -The main benefits of resource pooling are, in order, (1) increased robustness against component failures; (2) better ability to handle localized surges in traffic; (3) maximized utilization.

Concept note-3: -Expert-Verified Answer. Virtualization is related to pooling and sharing in a cloud concept. Through this, the resources are shared. Through this type of computation, it is possible to run applications and services on a common distributed network.

Concept note-4: -Private cloud-The cloud infrastructure is provisioned for exclusive use by a single organization comprising multiple consumers. It may be owned, managed, and operated by the organization, a third party, or some combination of them, and it may exist on or off premises.

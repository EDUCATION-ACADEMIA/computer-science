one drive is given by

(A) Google

(B) apple

(C) ** microsoft

(D) windows

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -OneDrive is the Microsoft cloud service that connects you to all your files. It lets you store and protect your files, share them with others, and get to them from anywhere on all your devices.

Concept note-2: -Microsoft OneDrive (formerly SkyDrive) is a file hosting service operated by Microsoft.

Concept note-3: -OneDrive is part of Microsoft Office 365 that can be used on Windows or Mac computers and Android or iOS devices. It provides 5GB of free cloud drive storage for registered users. Both Microsoft OneDrive and Google Drive are great for you to try.

Concept note-4: -OneDrive is an integral part of Office 365. OneDrive is a Microsoft-hosted location where employees can store and share files and access files from anywhere using any Internet-connected device.

Concept note-5: -Microsoft OneDrive and Google Drive are the two most popular cloud storage services out there. Although they essentially offer the same service, the approach is radically different.

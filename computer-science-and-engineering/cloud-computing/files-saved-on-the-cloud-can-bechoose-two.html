Files saved on the cloud can be ____ (choose two)

(A) ** Downloaded to your device

(B) Uploaded to your device

(C) Deleted from your device

(D) ** Deleted from the cloud

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can delete files you no longer want to store in iCloud Drive. Important: When you delete a file from iCloud Drive on iCloud.com, itâ€™s also deleted from all your devices that have iCloud Drive turned on.

Concept note-2: -The short answer is yes. After you delete a file from your computer, the file will also be deleted from the cloud the next time your system syncs. At the same time, this is not a permanent, irreversible process. Most cloud storage systems allow you to restore a deleted file within 30 days.

Concept note-3: -All deleted files go into the Recycle bin, where you have a chance to restore it.

Concept note-4: -Right-click the item or items, then select Move to Trash.

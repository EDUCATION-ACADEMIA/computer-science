Managed IT services are based on the concept of which one of the following?

(A) Virtualization

(B) ** Utility Computing

(C) Grid Computing

(D) Service Oriented Architecture (SOA)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -8) Managed IT services are based on the concept of which one of the following? Explanation: In Cloud Computing, managed IT services and grid computing are based on the concept of Utility Computing.

Concept note-2: -Utility computing is a model in which computing resources are provided to the customer based on specific demand. The service provider charges exactly for the services provided, instead of a flat rate.

Concept note-3: -Managed IT services are tasks handled by a third party, frequently in the context of business information technology services. Concept note-5: -Utility computing solutions can include virtual servers, virtual storage, virtual software, backup and most IT solutions. Cloud computing, grid computing and managed IT services are based on the concept of utility computing.

CLOUD COMPUTING

[SOURCES]
Applications which runs in cloud

(A) ** e-mail

(B) Google drive

(C) iCloud

(D) ** Web conferencing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Web conferencing is any type of online meeting that involves two or more participants in different locations. These participants could be anywhere in the world. With a reliable internet connection and conferencing software, they can see, talk, and hear each other in real-time.

Concept note-2: -Zoom is the ideal web meeting software for users who want to simply and effectively connect with customers and clients remotely. It also has Chrome and Linux OS support for increased flexibility and secure socket layer (SSL) encryption to guarantee secure communications.

Concept note-3: -Zoom. Zoom is popular choice for many businesses and offers several plan options. The “Basic” option is free, offers unlimited 1 to 1 meetings yet limits calls to 40 minutes.

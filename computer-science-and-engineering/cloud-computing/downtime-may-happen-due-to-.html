[SOURCES]
Downtime may happen due to ____

(A) loss of power

(B) low internet connectivity

(C) data centres going out of service for maintenance

(D) ** All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This can be due to several reasons including hardware or software failure, human error, malicious attacks or natural disasters. Since unplanned downtime is unexpected and occurs without a warning, preventing it can be a challenge.

Concept note-2: -Please be advised that there will be scheduled downtime across our network from (date/time) to (date/time). This is because we are performing work on (functions/parts of the network). We apologize for any inconvenience. For more information, or if you have any questions, please contact the IT Team at (contact details).

Concept note-3: -Example Sentences After a busy day at work, I look forward to some downtime at home. The kids napped during their downtime. We need to minimize network downtime.

CLOUD COMPUTING

[SOURCES]
The group of people who use digital devices and embrace the newest technology is known as?

(A) Digital lower class

(B) ** Digital middle class

(C) Digital upper class

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -digital middle class. Group of people who extensively use digital devices and embrace the newest digital technologies. digital revolution. Ever-expanding progression of technical, economic, and cultural changes brought about by computers. e-mail.

Concept note-2: -Augmented reality (AR) is an enhanced version of the real physical world that is achieved through the use of digital visual elements, sound, or other sensory stimuli and delivered via technology.

Concept note-3: -Computer engineering is defined as the discipline that embodies the science and technology of design, construction, implementation, and maintenance of software and hardware components of modern computing systems and computer-controlled equipment.

Concept note-4: -Cloud computing is named as such because the information being accessed is found remotely in the cloud or a virtual space. Companies that provide cloud services enable users to store files and applications on remote servers and then access all the data via the Internet.

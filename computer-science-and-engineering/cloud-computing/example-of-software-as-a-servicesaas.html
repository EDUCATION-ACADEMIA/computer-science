Example of Software as a service(SaaS)

(A) AT&T-hosting

(B) Rackspace

(C) ** Gmail

(D) AmazonEC2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Applications like email (Gmail, Yahoo mail etc), Social Networking sites (Facebook etc)

Concept note-2: -Well-known SaaS examples include Dropbox, Google Workspace, and Salesforce. Infrastructure-as-a-service (IaaS) offers access to resources such as servers, storage, memory, and other services. It allows organizations to purchase resources as needed.

Concept note-3: -Gmail is a free email service provided by Google. As of 2019, it had 1.5 billion active users worldwide. A user typically accesses Gmail in a web browser or the official mobile app. Google also supports the use of email clients via the POP and IMAP protocols.

Concept note-4: -From a network that spans the globe to innovative solutions that transform organizations, Google Cloud has SaaS built into our DNA.

Concept note-5: -Google Cloud is a suite of cloud computing services that runs on the same infrastructure that Google uses internally for their own consumer products, such as Google Search, Gmail, and YouTube. The list of available Google Cloud services is long-and it keeps growing.

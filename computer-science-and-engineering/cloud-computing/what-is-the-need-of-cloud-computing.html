What is the Need of Cloud Computing?

(A) ** Maintenance, Cost, Performance, Security

(B) Cost, Performance, Storage

(C) Performance, Cost, Security, Storage

(D) Performance, Storage, Security

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The cloud offers businesses more flexibility overall versus hosting on a local server. And, if you need extra bandwidth, a cloud-based service can meet that demand instantly, rather than undergoing a complex (and expensive) update to your IT infrastructure.

Concept note-2: -Other Important Benefits of Cloud Computing Offers Resilient Computing. Fast and effective virtualization. Provide you low-cost software. Offers advanced online security.

Concept note-3: -The three main types of cloud deployment models are private, public, or hybrid.

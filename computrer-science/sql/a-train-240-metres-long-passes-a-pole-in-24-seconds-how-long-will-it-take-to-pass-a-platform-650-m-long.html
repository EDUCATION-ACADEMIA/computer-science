A train 240 metres long passes a pole in 24 seconds. How long will it take to pass a platform 650 m long?

(A) 65 sec

(B) ** 89 sec

(C) 100 sec

(D) 150 sec

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We have given a 240 m long train and the time it takes to cross a pole is 24 seconds and we are asked to find the time that the same train will take to cross a 650 m platform. Hence, we got the speed of the train as 10 m/s. From the above calculations, the train will take 89 seconds to cross the platform.

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer. Answer: Required time is 10.8 seconds.

Concept note-3: -Correct Answer: C) 18 metres/sec. We know that when a train crosses a platform, it covers a distance equal to the sum of its length and that of platform.

Concept note-4: -∴ The speed of the train is 72 km/hr.

Concept note-5: -Length of platform = total distance covered – length of the train. ∴ Length of the platform is 375 meter.

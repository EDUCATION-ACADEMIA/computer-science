To find the total dollar value of sales, use which?

(A) ** SUM

(B) COUNT

(C) MAX

(D) MIN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To calculate the total values of sales, multiply the average price per product or service sold by the number of products or services sold.

Concept note-2: -The sales revenue formula calculates revenue by multiplying the number of units sold by the average unit price. Service-based businesses calculate the formula slightly differently: by multiplying the number of customers by the average service price.

Concept note-3: -Enter “=SUM(D1:D#)” in the next empty cell in column D. Replace “#” with the row number of the last entry in column D. In the example, enter “=SUM(D1:D2)” to calculate the total sales revenue for the two items.

Concept note-4: -Total sales or gross sales is the grand total of all sales revenues a business generates from normal activities. This is a vital number for any business because money has to go into the cash register before it can be used to pay bills and provide profit for investors.

In how many ways can a group of 5 men and 2 women be made out of a total of 7 men and 3 women?

(A) ** 63

(B) 90

(C) 126

(D) 45

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Out of 7 men and 3 women, a group of 5 men and 2 women can be made by 7C5×3C2=63.

Concept note-2: -In how many ways can a group of 5 men and 2 women be made out of a total of 7 men and 3 women? = 63.

Concept note-3: -If i multiply the permutation of these groups by the permutation of women inside this group i would get a total of 5! * 3! = 720. I thought of using this strategy for the problem of arranging the men and multiplying by C(4, 3)=4 because the group is a combination of 3 out of 4 men, which would give 4 * 720 = 2880.

Concept note-4: -Hence, the answer total number of ways to form a 5 member committee is 756.

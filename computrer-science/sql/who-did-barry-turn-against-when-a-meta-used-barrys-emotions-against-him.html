Who did Barry turn against when a meta used Barry’s emotions against him?

(A) ** Arrow

(B) Vibe

(C) Reverse Flash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eobard Thawne, otherwise known as the Reverse-Flash, is a character in The CW’s Arrowverse franchise. He first appeared in the pilot episode of the television series The Flash, and has also been featured in various spin-off shows and crossover events set in the shared fictional universe.

Concept note-2: -Fearful that history was repeating itself, Barry killed Thawne by breaking his neck.

Concept note-3: -Similar to what Thawne himself said in the episode, there’s always been this sense that being evil is a part of who he is. But now it seems that the Negative Speed Force, combined with the fact that he had no one in his life to keep him from going over the edge, is what made him a full-fledged villain.

Concept note-4: -Wells killed Barry’s mother, but as he told Cisco, he actually wanted to kill Barry that night 15 years ago. Viewers do not know why Wells wanted to kill Barry, but at this point, all he cares about his getting home, though if he can kill Barry in the process that’s a bonus.

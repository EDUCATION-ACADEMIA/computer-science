A and B can complete a work in 6 days.A can alone do it in 10 days.Then B can alone do the work in how many days?

(A) 13.75 days

(B) 14 days

(C) ** 15 days

(D) 16 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -so, we can say that B will take 18 days to complete the same work.

Concept note-2: -Hence, A requires 10 days to finish the work alone and B requires 15 days to finish the work. So, the correct answer is “10 days”.

Concept note-3: -So, both together will finish the work in 6 days.

Concept note-4: -They worked together for 2 days and then B left the work. How many days will A require to finish the work? ∴ A can finish the work in 2 ½ days.

Concept note-5: -∴ A and B can do the work in 6 days.

Which of the following is TRUE about Random Forest and Adaboost?

(A) Order of trees matters in Random Forest

(B) ** Order of trees matters in Adaboost

(C) Order of trees does not matter in Random Forest and Adaboost

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following is/are true about Random Forest and Gradient Boosting ensemble methods? Both algorithms are design for classification as well as regression task.

Concept note-2: -Here are two main differences between Random Forest and AdaBoost: Decision Trees in Random Forest have equal contributions to the final prediction, whereas Decision Stumps in AdaBoost have different contributions, i.e. some stumps have more say than others.

Concept note-3: -QUESTION 1 Which of the following statements is true about random forests Random forests are an ensemble method. They combine and average the predictions from large number of trees.

Concept note-4: -Which of the following statements is/are correct regarding AdaBoost?-Adaboost builds weak learners (decision trees) with restricted depth. Since the decision trees are weak learners, therefore they are mainly one-step or two-step decision trees (restricted depth).

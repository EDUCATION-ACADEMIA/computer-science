Concept note-1: -The correlation coefficient is used to identify the strength of the linear relationship between two variables.

Concept note-2: -When interpreting a correlation coefficient, it is important to look at: The +/– sign of the correlation coefficient.

Concept note-3: -Just as a partial regression coefficient shows the relationship of y to one of the independent variables, holding constant the other variables, a partial correlation coefficient measures the strength of the relationship between y and one of the independent variables, holding constant all other variables in the model.

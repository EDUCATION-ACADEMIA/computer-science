Another name for a terminator in a flow chart is ____

(A) ** start / end

(B) decision

(C) process

(D) data input / output

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Also known as an “Action Symbol, ” this shape represents a process, action, or function. It’s the most widely-used symbol in flowcharting. Also known as the “Terminator Symbol, ” this symbol represents the start points, end points, and potential outcomes of a path. Often contains “Start” or “End” within the shape.

Concept note-2: -The oval, or terminator, is used to represent the start and end of a process.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Start/End Symbol. A good flow chart should clearly and effectively depict the process. It’s good to focus on a few things at first.

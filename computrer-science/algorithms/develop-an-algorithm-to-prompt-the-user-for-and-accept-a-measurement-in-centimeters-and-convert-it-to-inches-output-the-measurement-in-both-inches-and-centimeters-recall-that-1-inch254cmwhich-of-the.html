EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To convert inches to centimetres, multiply the given inch value by 2.54 centimetres. For example, to convert 7 inches to centimeters, multiply 7 by 2.54. Therefore, 7 inches is equal to 17.78 cm.

Concept note-2: -To convert cm to inches, divide your cm figure by 2.54 or multiply it by 0.3937. There are 2.54 cm in 1 inch.

Concept note-3: -One inch is equivalent to 2.54 cm.

For this array of numbers:[1, 2, 3, 4, 5]How many comparisons are made using Selection Sort?

(A) 5

(B) ** 10

(C) 1

(D) 6

(E) 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -10 should be the correct answer.

Concept note-2: -For 10 data items, this is 45 comparisons.

Concept note-3: -In general, the average number of comparisons per pass in selection sort will always be one half of the number of items to be sorted. For eight items, we have 1/2(82 + 8) = 1/2(64 + 8) = 1/2(72) = 36 comparisons.

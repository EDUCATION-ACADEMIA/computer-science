Concept note-4: -The Raw Facts and Figures are Called Data. The word raw means that the facts have not yet been processed to get their exact meaning. Data is collected from different sources.

Concept note-5: -Processed data is called information. Information is the result of processing raw data. Data is raw and unprocessed, while information is a processed version of data. Information can be stored in many forms, such as text, images, sound, and videos.

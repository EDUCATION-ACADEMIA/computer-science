____ is a multimedia file format used to store videos and audios.

(A) ** MP4

(B) MP3

(C) GIF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MP4 (MPEG-4 Part 14) is the most common type of video file format. Appleâ€™s preferred format, MP4 can play on most other devices as well. It uses the MPEG-4 encoding algorithm to store video and audio files and text, but it offers lower definition than some others.

Concept note-2: -The most commonly used containers for media on the web are probably MPEG-4 (MP4), QuickTime Movie (MOV), and the Wavefile Audio File Format (WAV). However, you may also encounter MP3, Ogg, WebM, AVI, and other formats.

Concept note-3: -MPEG-4 Part 14 or MP4 is a digital multimedia container format most commonly used to store video and audio, but it can also be used to store other data such as subtitles and still images. Like most modern container formats, it allows streaming over the Internet.

Concept note-4: -MP4 is a video format, but can also be used for audio.

Concept note-5: -MP4 is a popular video format used to share, download, and stream video content. In addition to video, the format can store audio, images, and also subtitles. It is a container format, meaning it can store various types of audio and video, as long as they are encoded in a codec supported by the MP4 format.

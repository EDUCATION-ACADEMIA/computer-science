In computer subtraction is generally carried out by

(A) 10’s complement

(B) 1’s complement

(C) ** 2’s complement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 2’s complement.

Concept note-2: -Binary subtraction is one of the four binary operations, where we perform the subtraction method for two binary numbers (comprising only two digits, 0 and 1). This operation is similar to the basic arithmetic subtraction performed on decimal numbers in Maths.

Concept note-3: -Complements are used in the digital computers in order to simplify the subtraction operation and for the logical manipulations.

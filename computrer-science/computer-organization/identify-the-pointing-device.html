Identify the pointing device

(A) Keyboard

(B) Mouse

(C) Light pen

(D) ** Touch sensitive screen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A touch screen is an electronic display screen that is also an input device. A user interacts with the computer, tablet, smartphone or touch-controlled appliance by using hand gestures and fingertip movements to tap pictures, moving elements or type words on the screen.

Concept note-2: -The major pointing devices in use today are the mouse, trackpad or touchpad (laptop), touch screens, etc.

Concept note-3: -Touch screens are used on various devices, such as computer and laptop displays, smartphones, tablets, cash registers, and information kiosks.

Concept note-4: -The various devices, such as smartphones, information kiosks, computers, laptop displays, tablets, and cash registers, all have a touch screen. Instead than using touch-sensitive input, some touch screens use a grid of infrared beams to detect the presence of a finger.

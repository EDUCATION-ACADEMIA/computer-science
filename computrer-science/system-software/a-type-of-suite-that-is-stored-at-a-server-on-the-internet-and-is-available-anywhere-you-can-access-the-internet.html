A type of suite that is stored at a server on the Internet and is available anywhere you can access the internet.

(A) Database

(B) Email

(C) ** Cloud

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cloud computing is named as such because the information being accessed is found remotely in the cloud or a virtual space. Companies that provide cloud services enable users to store files and applications on remote servers and then access all the data via the Internet.

Concept note-2: -Simply put, the cloud is the Internet-more specifically, it’s all of the things you can access remotely over the Internet. When something is in the cloud, it means it’s stored on Internet servers instead of your computer’s hard drive.

Concept note-3: -Cloud suites or online office suites are stored at a server on the Internet and are available anywhere you can access the Internet. Office suites, also known as office software suites and productivity suites, contain general-purpose application programs that are typically used in a business situation.

Concept note-4: -The term “cloud services” refers to a wide range of services delivered on demand to companies and customers over the internet. These services are designed to provide easy, affordable access to applications and resources, without the need for internal infrastructure or hardware.

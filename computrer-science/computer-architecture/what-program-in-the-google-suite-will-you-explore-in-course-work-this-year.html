What program in the Google Suite will you explore in course work this year?

(A) Google Expeditions

(B) Google Hangouts

(C) Google Groups

(D) ** Google Drawings

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google Drawings is available as a web application and as a desktop application on Googleâ€™s ChromeOS. The app allows users to create and edit flowcharts, organisational charts, website wireframes, mind maps, concept maps, and other types of diagrams online while collaborating with other users in real-time.

Concept note-2: -Google Drawings is a virtual board that allows users to create content by drawing, pasting images, linking videos and websites, and inserting text, shapes, tables, and other content onto a page in Google Drive. Users can create flow charts, diagrams, concept maps, visual storyboards, original art and more.

Concept note-3: -You access your Google Drive on the web through this URL: Click on New in the top left-hand corner. Then click on More and then Google Drawings. Alternatively, you can type https://docs.google.com/drawings into your browser or access Drawings via the Chrome app.

Concept note-4: -You can draw in Google Docs by using the built-in Drawing tool. The Drawing tool allows you to create and manipulate lines, shapes, colors, text, and more to add an artistic touch to your documents.

Which of the following is the largest computer network?

(A) Large Area Network (LAN)

(B) Local Area Network (LAN)

(C) Metropolitan Area Network (MAN)

(D) ** Wide Area Network (WAN)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The world’s largest WAN is the internet because it is a collection of many international networks that connect to each other.

Concept note-2: -The Internet is the world’s largest computer network.

Concept note-3: -WANs are the largest and most expansive forms of computer networks available to date.

Concept note-4: -A WAN will typically cover a larger area geographically, such as a continent, a state or a country. A LAN connects computers within a small and specific area geographically. A MAN is confined to a specific town, city or region. It covers a larger area than a LAN but a smaller area than a WAN.

Concept note-5: -The internet is the largest network in the world.

[SOURCES]
Hardware such as computers, servers, printers, fax machines, switches, and routers.

(A) ** Device

(B) Physical Media

(C) Network Adapter

(D) Computer network

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Routers, hubs, switches, and bridges are some examples of network hardware.

Concept note-2: -Hardware devices that are used to connect computers, printers, fax machines and other electronic devices to a network are called network devices. These devices transfer data in a fast, secure and correct way over same or different networks. Network devices may be inter-network or intra-network.

Concept note-3: -The hardware components are the server, client, peer, transmission medium, and connecting devices. The software components are operating system and protocols.

Concept note-4: -1. A modem or broadband modem is a hardware device that connects a computer or router to a broadband network.

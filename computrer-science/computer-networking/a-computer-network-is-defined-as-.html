A computer network is defined as ____

(A) 2 computers connected together

(B) ** 2 or more computers connected together

(C) A large number of computers connected together

(D) All the computers connected together

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A network consists of two or more computers that are linked in order to share resources (such as printers and CDs), exchange files, or allow electronic communications. The computers on a network may be linked through cables, telephone lines, radio waves, satellites, or infrared light beams.

Concept note-2: -SUMMARY. When two or more computers are connected together so they can communicate with one another, they form a network. The largest computer network in the world in the Internet.

Concept note-3: -Computer networking refers to interconnected computing devices that can exchange data and share resources with each other.

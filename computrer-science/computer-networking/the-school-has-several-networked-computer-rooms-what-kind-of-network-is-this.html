The school has several networked computer rooms. What kind of network is this?

(A) ** LAN

(B) WAN

(C) Mesh

(D) GAN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A local area network (LAN) connects personal computers, printers, and other computer resources together within a building or campus.

Concept note-2: -A LAN network connects devices in one building or even two and can also be found in schools or laboratories.

Concept note-3: -A school network is usually a LAN. LANs are often connected to WANs, for example a school network could be connected to the internet.

Concept note-4: -There are two primary LAN types: wired LANs and wireless LANs (WLANs). A wired LAN uses switches and Ethernet cabling to connect endpoints, servers and internet of things (IoT) devices to the corporate network.

Concept note-5: -A Local Area Network (LAN) is among the most popular types of computer networks. A LAN network connects a group of computers and peripheral devices in a specific area, such as a school, laboratory, home, or office building.

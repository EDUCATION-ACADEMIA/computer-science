Thomas Mills High School has computers being used in a number of school buildings within the site.Which network is being used?

(A) ** LAN

(B) WAN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: LAN : It is Local Area Network.

Concept note-2: -A campus area network (CAN) is a computer network that spans a limited geographic area. CANs interconnect multiple local area networks (LAN) within an educational or corporate campus. Most CANs connect to the public Internet.

Concept note-3: -Local area network (LAN) A LAN is an interconnected system limited in size and geography. It typically connects computers and devices within a single office or building. It is used by small companies or as a test network for small-scale prototyping.

Concept note-4: -Last Updated: February 10, 2022. A local area network (LAN) is defined as a connected environment spanning one or more buildings – typically in a 1-kilometer radius – that links computing devices within close proximity of each other by using ethernet and Wi-Fi technology.

Concept note-5: -A local area network (LAN) is a computer network that interconnects computers within a limited area such as a residence, school, laboratory, university campus or office building.

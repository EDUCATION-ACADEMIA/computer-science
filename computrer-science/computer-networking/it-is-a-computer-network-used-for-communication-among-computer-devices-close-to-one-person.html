It is a computer network used for communication among computer devices close to one person?

(A) ** Peer to Peer Network

(B) Metropolitan Area Network

(C) Wide Area Network

(D) Local Area Network

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A personal area network (PAN) is a computer network used for communication among computers and different information technological devices close to one person.

Concept note-2: -Personal area network – A personal area network (PAN) is a computer network used for communication among computer and different information technological devices close to one person.

Concept note-3: -In a peer-to-peer network, computers on the network are equal, with each workstation providing access to resources and data. This is a simple type of network where computers are able to communicate with one another and share what is on or attached to their computer with other users.

Concept note-4: -1 Answer. A computer network which is used for communication among computers and their peripherals in small area to each other is called LAN.

True or False-a Router and a Modem can be purchased as one combined device.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Modem router combos conveniently combine an internet modem and Wi-Fi router into an all-in-one device, thus making it a WiFi networking essential. Cable modem router combos are often provided by the cable company for a fee that is part of your monthly bill.

Concept note-2: -Are you confused about getting a modem router combo or a standalone modem + separate router? Well, modem router combos are cheaper in price, but the separate devices can be a bit expensive or cheaper depending on the setup you choose. However, separate devices offer better performance and flexibility than a combo.

Concept note-3: -Connect an Ethernet cable to the “Ethernet” port on your modem. This port is used to connect other devices to your modem. Connect the other end of the cable to the router. Use the other end of the Ethernet cable to connect to the port labeled “WAN", “Internet", or something similar.

Concept note-4: -Today’s technology has evolved so that we now have what is called a gateway. The gateway combines the function of both the modem and the router into one handy device. That way there’s only one device to plug in and configure.

[SOURCES]
The NOR gate output will be high if the two inputs are ____

(A) ** 00

(B) 01

(C) 10

(D) 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The output of NOR gate is high when all inputs are low. Explanation: As we see the truth table for NOR gate : When both the input is low (0), then output is high (1).

Concept note-2: -The NOR gate is a digital logic gate that implements logical NOR-it behaves according to the truth table to the right. A HIGH output (1) results if both the inputs to the gate are LOW (0); if one or both input is HIGH (1), a LOW output (0) results.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. NOR gate: It is a digital circuit having two or more inputs but only one output. It gives a low output if either input A or B is high, otherwise, it gives a high output (1).

Concept note-4: -The output of an AND gate is HIGH only when all inputs are HIGH. The output of a NAND gate is HIGH whenever one or more inputs are LOW. The output of a NOR gate is LOW whenever one or more inputs are HIGH.

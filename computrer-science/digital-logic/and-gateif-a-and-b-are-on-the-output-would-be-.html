[SOURCES]
AND Gate:IF A and B are ON, the output would be ____

(A) ** ON

(B) OFF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The output of AND gate is given as AB hence its value is logic 1 when both inputs A and B are logic 1’s.

Concept note-2: -In Boolean algebra, output of AND gate is written as Y=A.

Concept note-3: -When the inputs are 1 and 0, the output is zero.

Concept note-4: -The XOR and XNOR gates are the hybrids gates. The 2-input OR gate is also known as the Inclusive-OR gate because when both inputs A and B are set to 1, the output comes out 1(high). In the Ex-OR function, the logic output “1” is obtained only when either A="1” or B="1” but not both together at the same time.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. Key Points: 1) The output is low or ‘0’ when both the inputs are the same. Hence XOR gate can be used to know whether two digital inputs are identical.

IC 7432 is for which of the following gate?

(A) NOR

(B) ** OR

(C) AND

(D) EX-OR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Description. The 7432 is a 14 Pin Quad 2-Input OR Gate IC. The 7432 provides four independent 2-input OR gates with standard push-pull outputs. The device is designed for operation with a power supply range of 2.0V to 6.0V.

Concept note-2: -7432 Quad 2-Input OR Gate is a Dual Input OR Gate with the Quad package. It contains four independent gates each of which performs the logic OR function. Each gate has two inputs thatâ€™s why it is named Quad 2-Input OR Gate.

Concept note-3: -The CD4071 OR gate IC is one kind of CMOS chip including 4-OR gates. Every OR gate includes 2-inputs & there are 4 logic gates incorporated on the IC. So, it is frequently called a Quad two-input OR gate.

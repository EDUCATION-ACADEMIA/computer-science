____ runs external devices

(A) device runners

(B) ** device drivers

(C) device instructors

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Device drivers must receive advice from the operating system to access and execute device instructions. After completing the job, they transmit the hardware deviceâ€™s output or message to the operating system. Devices such as modems, routers, speakers, keyboards, and printers require device drivers to operate.

Concept note-2: -There are various types of device drivers for I/O devices such as keyboards, mice, CD/DVD drives, controllers, printers, graphics cards and ports.

Concept note-3: -Purpose. The main purpose of device drivers is to provide abstraction by acting as a translator between a hardware device and the applications or operating systems that use it. Programmers can write higher-level application code independently of whatever specific hardware the end-user is using.

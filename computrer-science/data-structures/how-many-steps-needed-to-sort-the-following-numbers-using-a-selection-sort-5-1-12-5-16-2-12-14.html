(B) ** 6

(C) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -What will be the number of passes to sort the elements using insertion sort? Explanation: The number of passes is given by N-1. Here, N=6.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: Even though the first two elements are already sorted, bubble sort needs 4 iterations to sort the given array.

(A) + 5 6

(B) ** 5 6 +

(C) 6 + 5

(D) 6 + 5 +

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -hHow do we convert it to postfix notation. For example, the infix expression (2+3)*(4+5) in postfix notation is 23+45+* and the infix expression 2+3*4+5 in postfix notation is 234*+5+. Also, since our four operators are left associative, 2 + 3 + 4 translates to 23+4+ and not 234++.

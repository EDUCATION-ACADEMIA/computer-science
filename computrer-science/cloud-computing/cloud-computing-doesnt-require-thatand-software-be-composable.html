EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hardware. Although you probably think of clouds as being virtual, they require hardware as part of the infrastructure. A cloud network is made up of a variety of physical hardware that can be located at multiple geographical locations.

Concept note-2: -A platform is a cloud computing service that is both hardware and software. Platforms are used to create more complex software. Virtual appliances are an important example of a platform, and they are becoming a very important standard cloud computing deployment object.

Concept note-3: -Cloud computing is a general term for anything that involves delivering hosted services over the internet. These services are divided into three main categories or types of cloud computing: infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS) and software as a service (SaaS).

What can be offered as a part of IaaS?

(A) application software

(B) call-center services

(C) ** computing capacity

(D) consulting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IaaS providers also offer additional services, such as detailed billing management, logging, monitoring, storage resiliency, and security. You can access IaaS resources using a pay-as-you-go basis, allowing you to only pay to consume the resources that you need.

Concept note-2: -IaaS is broken into three main components: compute, network, and storage.

Concept note-3: -Characteristics of IaaS systems include: Automated administrative tasks. Dynamic scaling. Platform virtualization technology.

Concept note-4: -In the IaaS model, users handle the applications, data, operating system, middleware, and runtimes. The IaaS vendor provides the virtualization, storage, network, and servers.

Concept note-5: -Self-Service Provisioning: Access via simple internet connection. Reallocate IT Resources: Free up IT staff for higher value projects. Reduce Downtime: IaaS enables instant recovery from outages. Boost Speed: Developers can begin projects once IaaS machines are provisioned.

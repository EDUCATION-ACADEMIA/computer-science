CLOUD COMPUTING

[SOURCES]
Cloud Computing depend on?

(A) Mother Board

(B) Application Software

(C) Specific Hardware

(D) ** Internet and Web Services

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For it to be considered “cloud computing, ” you need to access your data or your programs over the internet, or at the very least, have that data synced with other information over the web.

Concept note-2: -Web services are the tools that allow users to interact with software over the Internet. Cloud services are the servers that store the data, security and other infrastructure pieces needed to allow Web services to provide unique value as Web-accessible applications.

Concept note-3: -Public cloud Public clouds are owned and operated by a third-party cloud service providers, which deliver their computing resources, like servers and storage, over the Internet.

Concept note-4: -Simply put, the cloud is the Internet-more specifically, it’s all of the things you can access remotely over the Internet. When something is in the cloud, it means it’s stored on Internet servers instead of your computer’s hard drive.

(A) Coding

(B) ** Testing

(C) Design

(D) Implementation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Hardware and software components are tested individually. Testing stage in which individual components are integrated and ensured that they are error-free to meet customer requirements.

Concept note-2: -A step in waterfall model that involves a meeting with the customer to understand the requirements. Clarification: A waterfall model involves requirement gathering as its first step. This is the most important phase. It is important to understand the customer requirements.

Concept note-3: -How many stages are there in process improvement? Explanation: Process measurement, analysis and change are the three stages.

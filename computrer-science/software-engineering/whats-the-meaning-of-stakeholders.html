what’s the meaning of stakeholders?

(A) ** people who are effect or affected positivley or negatively

(B) people are only customers and buy or sell items

(C) people who are called VCs and motivate the companies

(D) people are hold every thing on the company to make them feel happy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Stakeholder means any people or groups who are positively or negatively impacted by a project, initiative, policy or organisation. They could be internal (people within your organisation) or external (people outside of your organisation).

Concept note-2: -A positive stakeholder sees the project’s positive side and benefits from its success. These stakeholders help the project management team to complete the project successfully. On the other hand, a negative stakeholder sees the outcome and may be negatively impacted by the project or its outcome.

Concept note-3: -Stakeholders are the individuals and organizations who are actively involved in the project or whose interest may be positively or negatively affected as a result of project execution.

Concept note-4: -“Affected stakeholders” refers to all those who may be significantly affected by a company’s operations, products, services and supply chains. Business activities can be positive but may also potentially harm the agency, dignity and human rights of individuals and communities.

Concept note-5: -Negative stakeholders are those who will be, or perceive themselves to be, negatively affected by the project. They could be individuals, groups, or entire organizations and they exist for numerous reasons. Some seem to exist as though it’s the calling of their cantankerous personalities.

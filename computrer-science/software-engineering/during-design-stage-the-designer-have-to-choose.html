During design stage, the designer have to choose:

(A) ** Database

(B) ** Implementation language

(C) ** Hardware requirements

(D) Communication platform

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The hardware product development lifecycle can be divided into seven phases: Requirements or ideation→ Design→ Manufacturing→ Testing→ Distribution→ Use and maintenance→ Disposal.

Concept note-2: -Process design is usually divided into 6 or 7 stages that includes: ask, research, imagine, plan, create, test and improve. The requirements are discovered in the “ask” and “research” phases where they can guide the rest of the cycle.

Concept note-3: -The design phase, or pre-construction phase, for commercial construction is especially important because you want to ensure that every decision being made will maximize your investment. With a creative partner you can trust, this pre-construction phase will help set your project up for success.

Concept note-4: -In the design phase, one or more designs are developed, with which the project result can apparently be achieved. Depending on the subject of the project, the products of the design phase can include dioramas, sketches, flow charts, site trees, HTML screen designs, prototypes, photo impressions and UML schemas.

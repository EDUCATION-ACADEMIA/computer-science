What is the correct order of waterfall model?

(A) analysis, design, testing, construction, maintenance

(B) ** analysis, design, construction, testing, maintenance

(C) design, analysis, construction, maintenance, testing

(D) design, construction, analysis, maintenance, testing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Phases of waterfall project management differ from one project to another. But generally, you can group the activities of the waterfall approach into five stages: planning, design, implementation, verification, and maintenance.

Concept note-2: -The waterfall model and software development life cycle have six stages: requirements, analysis, design, coding, testing, and deployment.

From the following which quality deals with maintaining the quality of the software product?

(A) Quality assurance

(B) ** Quality control

(C) Quality efficiency

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Quality control deals with maintaining quality of software product-Program Writing.

Concept note-2: -Quality Control (QC) is the process through which you achieve, or improve, product quality. The QC process can also include the activities used to eliminate waste processes in the SDLC. QC functions involve a range of testing activities used to detect and resolve technical issues.

Concept note-3: -Software quality assurance (SQA) is a process that assures that all software engineering processes, methods, activities, and work items are monitored and comply with the defined standards.

Concept note-4: -A key concept of Quality control is that all work products have defined, measurable specifications to which we may compare the output of each process. The feedback loop is essential to minimize the defects produced.
